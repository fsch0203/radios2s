30-06-2017 09.56: Importing stations from radiobrowser.info

Make new stations.csv

Get all stations in JSON: http://www.radio-browser.info/webservice/json/stations
Convert to csv in https://json-csv.com/ or in http://www.convertcsv.com/json-to-csv.htm
Open in Excel
Delete columns except: id, name, url, tags, country, language, bitrate
Change titles: id, tit, u1, sty, cou, lan, btr
Save as csv
